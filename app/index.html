Hello there.
We are the Cooperative Interaction Lab.

We are an interdisciplinary group of researchers seeking to find implications for the design of software that empowers people to cooperate more effectively and creatively.

Our research interests include computer supported cooperative work, social informatics, global software development, OSS communities, web technologies, social media, software engineering education, among others.

We work as a development team and strive to be as open as possible to new ideas. We are located in Natal, a beautiful city in the northeast of Brazil. We'll be delighted to have you visiting!
